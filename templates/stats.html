Data Analysis Summary

This data is offering a detailed insight into crime statistics from the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK) province in Pakistan, spanning a period from 2011 to 2015. It encapsulates data across 7 distinct administrative divisions and covers 25 different districts within the province, reflecting a broad spectrum of the region's demographic. This datas categorizes crimes into 7 types, which include Murder, Kidnapping, Child Lifting, Abduction, Car Theft, Car Snatching, and Motorcycle Theft . Each entry in the dataset specifies the year, the division and district where the crime occurred, the type of crime, and the number of cases reported. This structured approach allows for a comprehensive analysis of crime trends and patterns across various regions and time periods within KPK. The dataset is a valuable resource for understanding the crime dynamics in the province and could be instrumental in developing strategies for law enforcement and public safety initiatives.
